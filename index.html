Company Overview

About My Queen Pty Ltd

Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Sydney, My Queen Pty Ltd is a premium food distribution and brand operation platform specializing in Asian food products.

Over the past decade, we've built a comprehensive sales and distribution network covering all of Australia, with self-operated warehousing and cold chain logistics centers in Sydney and Melbourne. We are not just a distributor — we are a growth engine for brands in the Australian market.
